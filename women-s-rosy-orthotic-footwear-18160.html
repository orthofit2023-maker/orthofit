Description

WHY ORTHOTIC FOOTWEAR:

It’s to create natural Alignment, improve gait & posture. This may help relieve common foot problems, enhance physical performance and avoid deformities. Studies show that 75 percent of people experience foot problems at some point in their lives. These problems are common in all age group from children to seniors, from top athletes to people with sedentary lifestyles.

LOSS OF YOUR “FOOTPRINT”

The human foot was originally designed to walk on natural, soft surfaces like earth and sand. Instead, we now spend most of the day on unnatural, hard, flat surfaces like pavements and hard floors.
This loss of our “footprint” has resulted in the feet flatten and invariably pronate (roll inwards) upon contact causing altered biomechanics lead to:

  • Heel Pain (Plantar fasciitis / Spur)

  • Achilles tendonitis (Retro calcaneum pain / Spur)

  • Ball of the foot pain / Metatarsalgia

  • Bunion (Hallux abducto valgus)

  • Knee Pain (Patellofemoral pain)

  • Lower back pain

  • Tibial stress syndrome

  • Iliotibial band syndrome


    •  

CLAIM BACK YOUR “FOOTPRINT”

Orthofit range of ready to wear orthotic footwear and custom insoles are designed to place under foot, the contours similar to a footprint. In doing so, it improves alignment and dispersion of peak pressures, common with lower limb biomechanical complaints.

Caution: As the Orthotic footwear realign your feet to neutral position, initially this corrects may give muscular discomfort.
